The Best Movies according to Reuben

My 3 top movies of all-times

Saving Private Ryan

I love the theme of personal sacrifice for a great cause.
I really like Private Ryan's way of thinking about living a life
worthy of the sacrifice others made to bring him home.

Hacksaw Ridge

The theme of personal sacrifice to save others in Desmond Doss
is due to his love for his personal savior: Jesus Christ.

La Cucaracha

Mexican film. Colonel Zeta must follow General Villa's orders
to organize an attack on a city to serve as bait, even if it
costs him his own life and that of his people.
